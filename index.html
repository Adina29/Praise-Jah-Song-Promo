What is 'Praise Jah In The Moonlight' about ?

"Praise Jah in the Moonlight" is a heartfelt song from YG Marley about faith and self-discovery. We can learn to maintain our faith, even during tough times as artist highlights in his lyrics ("Give Jah all the thanks and praises"). Remember that it's okay to love deeply. Rejection does not diminish your worth: "showed you I loved you; you called me a liar." You're never alone, reach out when feeling lonely. Communication enhances interconnectedness among us despite apparent isolation. Lastly, this song teaches us that everyone has intrinsic value beyond material measures. Respect yourself and others along these lines - “it’s worth more than gold”.
